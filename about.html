About Us

Heritage Meets
Innovation

Founded in 2017 in Bhilwara, Rajasthan's textile heartland, Sitaram Denims began with 8 Simco weaving looms and a vision for quality. Today, we stand as a professionally managed, NSE Emerge listed textile manufacturer with 10 years of continuous operation, blending traditional craftsmanship with modern governance.

Our journey reflects Rajasthan's textile legacy while embracing global standards. As a vertically integrated manufacturer, we control the entire process from yarn to finished fabric, ensuring uncompromising quality across Polyester-Viscose blends, denim, cotton, and specialty fabrics.

Today, we operate two state-of-the-art manufacturing units in Bhilwara with a skilled workforce of over 400 professionals, we combine generational textile expertise with cutting-edge technology, serving domestic and international markets while maintaining the trust we've built since 2017.

SEBI Compliant Governance: Transparent operations with dedicated board committees for audit, stakeholders, and governance reinforce our commitment to ethical manufacturing and investor confidence.
