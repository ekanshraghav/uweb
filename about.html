OUR STORY

Heritage of
Technical Mastery

Sitaram Denims India Limited was established in 1987 in Bhilwara, Rajasthan, as a professionally managed enterprise promoted by qualified textile engineers[citation:3]. The founders brought with them a strong legacy of operating textile enterprises, forming what is now known as the Sitaram Group[citation:1].

The company has grown into a vertically integrated textile manufacturer, managing the entire value chain from yarn twisting to weaving and packing finished fabrics[citation:1]. We specialize in cotton, polyester, viscose, denim, linen, and other allied fabrics, primarily for men's wear under our brand names[citation:1].

Today, with over 39 years of industry experience, we operate from advanced facilities in Rajasthan, exporting to more than 10 countries and maintaining a production capacity of over 35 million meters annually.
