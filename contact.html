Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our products, services, and processes.

What is your typical lead time for orders?

Our standard lead time is 4-6 weeks for regular orders, depending on the fabric type and quantity. Rush orders can be accommodated with advance notice and additional charges.

Do you offer custom dyeing services?

Yes, we provide custom dyeing services with our advanced color matching technology. We can match specific Pantone colors or provide samples based on your requirements.

What certifications do your products carry?

Our fabrics are certified under GOTS, OEKO-TEX Standard 100, and ISO 9001:2015. We also comply with various international safety and environmental standards.

Can you provide samples before bulk orders?

Absolutely! We encourage customers to request samples for quality evaluation. Our sample program allows you to test fabric properties before committing to larger orders.

What is your minimum order quantity?

Our MOQ varies by fabric type: 1,000 meters for regular denim, 500 meters for stretch denim, and 2,000 meters for specialty or custom fabrics. Contact us for specific requirements.
