Zero Liquid Discharge Systems

Our state-of-the-art water treatment facilities ensure that no wastewater is discharged into the environment. Through advanced reverse osmosis, ultrafiltration, and evaporation technologies, we achieve 100% water recycling.

This comprehensive approach has reduced our water consumption by 60% while improving water quality for all stakeholders.

Solar Power & Renewable Energy

Our commitment to clean energy is reflected in our 5 MW solar power installation, which now provides 25% of our total energy requirements. This sustainable initiative significantly reduces our carbon footprint while ensuring long-term energy security.

We continuously invest in renewable energy technologies and are working towards achieving 50% renewable energy usage by 2025.
